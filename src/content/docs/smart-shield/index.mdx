Safeguard your origin with just a few clicks.

Smart Shield acts as an intermediate caching layer between Cloudflare's content delivery network and your origin server, consolidating multiple requests from various locations into a single request. Combined with connection reuse, this approach significantly reduces origin load while improving your domain performance.
